01 · Concept

The high-level story: concept, positioning, guest, and menu.

Positioning & Guest

Aegean Modern is an upscale-casual, contemporary Greek restaurant & bar designed as a destination for:

  • Guests 28–65 with strong dining spend
  • Food-forward professionals, couples, and groups
  • Weekend brunch & late-night bar crowd
Modern Greek Wood-fire grill Mezze shared plates High-energy nights

Menu Direction

Core menu pillars:

  • Mezze: tzatziki trio, tyrokafteri, octopus, calamari, spanakopita
  • Mains: lamb chops, whole branzino, souvlaki, modern moussaka
  • Vegetarian: roasted cauliflower, halloumi, Mediterranean grain bowls
  • Desserts: loukoumades, baklava sundae, yogurt panna cotta
  • Bar: Greek spirits, citrus/herb cocktails, Greek wines, NA options

02 · Financials

Revenue model, cost structure, break-even, and key assumptions.

Revenue Model

  • Avg. check: $45–65 (dinner), $25–35 (brunch)
  • Bar share: 30–35% of revenue
  • Weekly covers: ~1,100–1,300
  • Year 1 revenue: $2.6M–$3.2M
  • Year 2–3 growth from optimization & maturation

Cost & Profitability

  • Food COGS: 27–30%
  • Beverage COGS: 18–22%
  • Labor: 30–33%
  • Occupancy: 6–10%
  • EBITDA: 8–12% Year 1; 12–17% thereafter

07 · Opening & April 1 Timeline

The restaurant is assumed fully built. This is the execution path to opening on April 1.

High-Level Timeline

  • January–February: finalize systems, manuals, and hiring pipeline.
  • Early March: leadership hired, core team signed, training prep.
  • Mid–Late March: full staff training, friends & family services.
  • Last week of March: soft opening and refinement.
  • April 1: grand opening with PR, influencers, VIPs.

Execution Focus

  • Hit bar % targets from day one with tight pour controls.
  • Lock SOPs early to protect guest experience under volume.
  • Use opening week as a controlled test of menu, pricing, and pacing.
  • Capture guest data (emails, loyalty) from the first service.

All related documents (opening checklists, staffing plans, grand opening playbook) are already linked above in Ops, People, and Marketing.
